About Me

A little about me.

I unknowingly started designing when I wanted a cover design for my watt-pad book and didn't know how to approach designers, so I designed one for myself. Surprisingly, I found the process very exhilarating, since then there is no looking back. I went from social media graphics to Info-graphics and now I have started with User Experience Design. I have worked and still working on an extensively diverse range of projects which operate on heterogeneous technologies.

Design & Development.

I continually test emerging tools and methods to find the most effective workflow. Side by side, try to learn more about web languages to create the most appealing experience. It's been quite a few time and now, I know my PNGs, PSDs, UI, UX, HTML, CSS and the frameworks.

My heart lies into research, also love the raw creation of mapping, sketches, flows, wireframes. And who doesn't approve of final mockups and testing process?

Design Goals.

According to the platform and requirement, I change the process but the principles are the same: aesthetics, simplicity and usability.

When designing for the screen, I gear everything around the user and business – it needs to look great, should be functional, no doubt about that, but it has to make sense for the business too.





I love working on self design projects as well as a challenging team project.

I love working with creative individuals that can bring strong critique as well as laugh at the latest meme. I thrive on the challenge of functional and visual design efforts that meet business and UX goals. I strive to take every opportunity to grow through teammates, trainings, industry events, read books and online resources.
